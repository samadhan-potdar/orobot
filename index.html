Innovative Solutions for Your Industry

THE TUBE PRO

Introducing Tube Pro by Orobot Technologies – a groundbreaking solution designed to revolutionize industrial cleaning. Specifically crafted for Heat Exchanger bodies in sugar factories, Tube Pro automates the labor-intensive cleaning process, replacing traditional methods with precision and efficiency. Its robotic design allows for variable diameter navigation, seamlessly moving vertically, horizontally, and around bends in pipes.
Equipped with specialized nozzles, Tube Pro performs tasks such as cleaning, painting, and inspection with unparalleled accuracy. The integration of a high-resolution camera ensures meticulous inspection, enhancing overall operational efficiency. Tube Pro is not just a product; it's a transformative step towards streamlined industrial processes, reducing downtime, minimizing costs, and ensuring sustainable operations. Welcome to the future of industrial cleaning with Tube Pro.

OUR SERVICES

Cleaning

Visual Inspection

Inspection service utilizes advanced robotic systems equipped with high-resolution cameras to conduct thorough examinations within heat exchangers, ensuring the safety and longevity of equipment.
